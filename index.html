Movie Review

Oppenheimer
Trailer

Oppenheimer is a great movie; I give it a 9/10 rating. I learned a lot about the history of World War II. I also learned about the science of an atomic and hyrdrogen bomb. The visuals are really well made, and the actor of Oppenheimer, Cillian Murphy, does a really good job.

Book Review

Lord of the Flies

Lord of the Flies, by William Golding, is a great novel that I read earlier this year. I give it an 8.5/10 rating. Golding does a great job using symbols, conveying leadership types through characters, and creating an allegory to World War II. He sets the scene very well as an isolated island with no way of escape, so they boys must find a way to stay alive. I really liked this book and thought it was awesome for freshman year.
